# **Raíces Cafe**

Official repository for Raíces Café, a specialty coffee shop located in Marinilla, Antioquia.

## **Live Site**

https://raices-cafe.vercel.app/

## **About the Project**

Raíces Café is a tribute to the land, tradition, and the passion behind every coffee bean grown in the region. This page serves as a digital home for customers to discover the origins of the cafe, view the menu offerings, and find the location.

## **Key features**

**Story & Philosophy:** An overview of the cafe connection to coffee farmers and preparation proccesses.

**Menu Showcase:** Highlights of signature coffee preparations and artisan pastries.

**Visitor Information:** Easy access to the location, hours of operation, and contact details.

## **Tech Stack:**

**Framework:** Astro 

**Deployment:** Vercel

**Styling:** CSS/Tailwind (Standard web styling)
